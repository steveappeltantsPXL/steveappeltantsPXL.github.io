04 / Approach
How I work
Leadership and engineering principles I hold to.
01

Reliability is the feature.

In healthcare, software failure has a cost measured in care, not clicks. I design for correctness, observability and graceful failure before anything else.

02

Lead by clearing the path.

Seventeen years of leadership taught me the job is to give people a clear problem, the context to solve it, and the cover to do it well — then get out of the way.

03

Standards & sustainability over heroics.

HL7, FHIR, reusable blueprints, open-source governance. Systems built on shared standards outlive any single clever solution — and keep working after the team changes.
